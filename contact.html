social media

You can click on (or copy the URI from) any of the social media logos on this page to find me on the respective social media platform(s). It is preferred that you find me on a free and decentralized platform (e.g. on my diaspora* or on my Mastodon). I do have a Reddit, but I really quite dislike Reddit, so if you do contact me on there, don’t be surprised if I take a while to respond. By the way, do not contact me using Reddit’s newfangled instant messaging system; “private messaging”/“direct messaging” and comments are just fine.

Be aware that the hyperlink to my diaspora* uses the web+diaspora: scheme/protocol, which requires your browser and/or operating system to have support for said protocol. If you don’t already have support, you can just take everything in the URI that comes after the web+diaspora:// to be my diaspora* identifier.

When emailing me, keep in mind that due to the mechanics of OpenPGP for email encryption, header data is not encrypted. Since the subject line of an email is part of its header, it’s generally best to keep the subject line blank; that way, less (potentially usable) data is sent unencrypted. If you don’t already have a way to send OpenPGP-encrypted emails, check out this page, which lists a variety of OpenPGP software for various platforms and use-cases. Note that that list is a wide survey, including non-free software; generally my first recommendation is Mailvelope, because it is free software and because web interfaces for email are so widespread nowadays.
